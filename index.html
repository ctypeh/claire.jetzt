Note on the Type

This website is typeset in Inter, an experimental and open-source typeface created in 2016 by Rasmus Andersson. Inter was designed specifically for computer-screens, with the intent of crafting highly-readable user interfaces. It's a beautifully clear but subtle typeface that departs from the all-encompassing use of Helvetica.
